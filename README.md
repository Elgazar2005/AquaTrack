# AquaTrack 🏊‍♂️

AquaTrack is a digital platform designed to help swimmers improve their performance and help coaches manage training more efficiently.  
The app focuses on tracking, analytics, communication, and structured training programs.

---

# 🚀 Features

## 1. Workout & Lap Tracking
Swimmers can log their daily training sessions.

**Features**
- Record laps, distance, and time
- Track stroke type (freestyle, butterfly, backstroke, breaststroke)
- Save training sessions
- View workout history

**Benefit**
Helps swimmers track their improvement over time.

---

## 2. Performance Analytics Dashboard
A visual dashboard that shows training progress.

**Features**
- Weekly and monthly performance charts
- Personal best times
- Average lap speed
- Training consistency tracking

**Benefit**
Allows swimmers and coaches to analyze performance trends.

---

## 3. Coach–Athlete Communication
A built-in communication system between swimmers and coaches.

**Features**
- Coach feedback on training sessions
- Messaging between coach and swimmer
- Training notes and suggestions
- Video analysis feedback

**Benefit**
Improves communication and training guidance.

---

## 4. Training Plan Generator
Helps coaches assign structured workouts.

**Features**
- Weekly training schedules
- Custom swim drills
- Difficulty levels
- Goal-based training plans

**Benefit**
Provides organized training programs instead of random workouts.

---

## 5. Goal Setting
Swimmers can define their training goals.

**Examples**
- Improve 100m freestyle time
- Increase weekly swimming distance
- Prepare for competitions

**Benefit**
Helps swimmers stay motivated and focused.

---

## 6. Progress Reports
Automatic reports generated by the system.

**Features**
- Monthly performance reports
- Training consistency score
- Progress summaries for coaches

**Benefit**
Gives clear insights into swimmer improvement.

---

## 7. Competition Preparation
Tools designed for swimmers preparing for races.

**Features**
- Track competition times
- Race preparation checklist
- Pre-competition training plans

---

## 8. Team Management (For Coaches)
A coach dashboard for managing athletes.

**Features**
- Manage multiple swimmers
- Assign training sessions
- Track team performance
- Compare swimmer progress

---

## 9. Notifications & Reminders
Helps swimmers stay consistent with training.

**Examples**
- Training reminders
- Goal progress alerts
- Coach feedback notifications

---

## 10. Wearable Integration (Advanced Feature)
Integration with smart devices.

**Examples**
- Connect with smart watches
- Automatic swim tracking
- Heart rate monitoring

---

# 📱 User Types

### Swimmers
- Track workouts and progress
- Set goals
- View analytics
- Communicate with coaches

### Coaches
- Manage swimmers
- Assign training plans
- Analyze performance
- Provide feedback

---

# 🎯 Project Goal

The goal of AquaTrack is to create a simple and powerful platform that improves swimmer performance and simplifies training management for coaches through data tracking, analytics, and communication tools.
